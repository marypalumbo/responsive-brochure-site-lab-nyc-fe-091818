The Best Thai Food in Murray Hill

New York City is well known for having amazing food from nearly every culture in the world. If you are a resident of the Murray Hill neighborhood, you may think that your options are limited if you want to grab a bite nearby. If you thought great Thai food could only be found in Thailand, think again! We've tried all the Thai restaurants in Murray Hill and found the best ones, so you don't have to!

Chaamlex

Although Chaamlex isn't technically in the Murray Hill neighborhood, it's worth the additional blocks. Delivery times are fast, food is always fresh, and great lunch specials!

See Menu

Thai-ish

The first time I had Thai-ish was a couple weeks after they opened and I ordered delivery. Their pineapple fried rice makes me come back for more every time.

See Menu

Lan Larb

A Murray Hill Thai food staple with a beautiful decor. Their extensive menu and quick delivery make Lan Larb a go to in my Thai food rotation.

See Menu

Maison Thai

This cozy spot is a great place for a date. Be sure to try the Garlic Prawns, they're perfectly cooked and extremely flavorful.

See Menu

Little Basil

This place is quite small, so you may be better off ordering delivery. Their Pad Thai is a must and their lunch specials are on point.

See Menu

Sabai

A relatively new Thai food restaurant in the area, Sabai is sure to please. More on the upscale side and a bit pricier than the others, but the food will leave you wanting more.
